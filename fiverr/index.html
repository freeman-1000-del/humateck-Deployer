YouTube Multilingual Channel High-Speed Register

Register titles and descriptions across dozens of countries in one structured workflow.

A Windows PC application for creators, agencies, and MCN teams who publish the same video to multiple language markets. This page describes the product only — no pricing, ads, or checkout here.

Open interactive demo Google OAuth setup guide

What it does

Multilingual metadata at scale

Prepare one source title and description, then apply localized versions across your selected country set — up to 70 supported markets.

Gemini-assisted translation workflow

Built-in prompt structure for Google Gemini chat translation. Copy, review, and paste back in a consistent format.

Google OAuth on your PC

Connect your own Google Cloud OAuth client. Credentials stay on your machine; registration uses your YouTube channel access.

Web demo vs PC app

The web demo shows translation flow and OAuth practice. Full YouTube registration runs only in the delivered PC desktop app.
